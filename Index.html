Guidelines

  • ID card is compulsory for entry.
  • Decisions made by judge will be final.
  • Decent behavior is expected; no rude, harsh behavior, or wrong actions will be tolerated.
  • Participants are requested to wear decent attire.
  • On-the-spot changes in the names of participants will not be allowed.
  • Participants and their accompanists must take care of their own belongings.
  • Organizers shall not be held responsible for any kind of loss.
  • Sequence of participation will be informed on the date of competition.
  • On-the-spot registration is allowed.
  • Participants should reach the venue 1 hour before the reporting time.
  • Any damage to the college property will result in the participant bearing the cost.
  • Results will be declared on the same day.
  • For further queries, please contact the respective event in charge.
  • Calls or queries may be entertained only between working hours from 12 pm to 6 pm.
  • Participants must reach the venue by 9:00 am onwards.
  • NOTE: Only one participant per event participation will be taken.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I register for the event?

A: You can register using the "Register" button in the guidelines section.

Q: Is an ID card necessary for entry?

A: Yes, an ID card is mandatory for entry into the event.

Q:Who can I contact for more information about a specific event?

A: For further queries, please contact the respective event in-charge during working hours from 12:00 pm to 6:00 pm.

Q: How many participants are allowed per event?

A: Only one participant per event is allowed to register and participate.

Q: How can I get the rules and regulations PDF for each event?

A: Once you register, you can click on the "Download" button next to the "Register" button to download the rules and regulations PDF.
